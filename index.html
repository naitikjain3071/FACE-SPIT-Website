About Us


F.A.C.E. is a techno-cultural committee of S.P.I.T. that is exclusively for the Computer Engineering Department. We aim to make the learning fun with various engaging workshops. Some of our flagship events Monsoon trek, our Signature FACE Cup, Branch Day strengthen the bonds of friendship among Computer students along with working as fun distractor to their fully loaded minds.

What's Next

IPL Auction 2020

12th & 13th September, 2020

With the IPL extravaganza beginning this month FACE SPIT gives you the opportunity to build your dream team at its own IPL AUCTION. The unique scoring system based on live performance of the players will definetly make your heart skip a beat and it will take your experience to a whole new level. Even a team with zero cricketing knowledge can beat the cricket pundits on their day.

App It Up - Phase 2

15th to 25th September, 2020

FACE SPIT in collaboration presents an App Development contest where participants are free to use whichever platform or framework they feel comfortable in. Also they will be assigned mentors to help them at any stage of the competition. Only the best projects will go into the final judging round where they'll present their idea and give a demo followed by a questionnaire by the judges.
